# Flashbots
[Flashbots](https://flashbots.net) - a research and development organization focusing on minimizing the negative externalities and risks of MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) in the blockchain ecosystem.

Flashbots is dedicated to advancing transparent and fair blockchain protocols, specifically addressing issues related to MEV to protect ecosystem integrity.

**Website** : [https://flashbots.net](https://flashbots.net)

**Documentation** : [https://docs.flashbots.net](https://docs.flashbots.net)
